You are spending money acquiring new guests while past guests who already love what you do sit untouched. This assessment shows you exactly where your revenue is waiting.

How to use this assessment: This is not a mindset exercise. It is not a planning document. It is designed to help you see clearly — where guest revenue already exists, what has momentum but no follow-through, and what can be reactivated without creating anything new. Be honest. Your Revenue Reality

recognition — or resistance.

You do not need to calculate a score. Use the descriptions below to orient yourself to where your guest revenue is actually sitting.

Unfinished, Still Alive
Several sections surfaced guest relationships that came close. Visits made. Interest expressed. Momentum that existed. The quiet that followed was never a closed door — it was an open one that no one walked back through. That changes now.
Warm but Untouched
The sections about lapsed guests and follow-up avoidance carried the most weight. The interest was real on both sides. Re-engagement felt uncomfortable. Silence filled the space — and over time, silence started to feel like an answer. It is not. These guests are still reachable.
Avoiding Return
The pattern showed up clearly: the pull toward something new is consistently stronger than the return to something already in motion. There is no shortage of opportunity here. There is a habit of moving away from it. Recognizing the habit is the beginning of changing it.
All three Revenue Realities share the same truth: the revenue is not blocked. It is waiting. Seeing the pattern clearly is the first step. Knowing how to move through it — with precision, not pressure — is the second.
